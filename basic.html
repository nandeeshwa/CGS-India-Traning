INSTITUTE OF AERONAUTICAL COLLEGE

The Institute of Aeronautical Engineering (IARE), established in 2000 and located in Hyderabad, India, is a prominent institution affiliated with Osmania University. Renowned for its focus on engineering and technology, IARE offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines, including Aeronautical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, and Computer Science Engineering. The institute boasts modern facilities, such as advanced laboratories and a comprehensive library, and emphasizes research and industry connections to enhance student learning and career opportunities. With strong accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), IARE is dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering innovation in engineering fields.
